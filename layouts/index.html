我们的教学方法

📖 阅读

阅读有助于提高英语的各个方面——词汇、拼写、语法和写作。它培养学习者对语言正确形式的直觉。阅读技能帮助学生掌握内容并得出结论。

✍️ 写作

写作为学习者提供成就的实际证据。它有助于巩固对词汇和结构的掌握，以及如何理解文本和撰写作文。它能培养学习者总结和自由运用语言的能力。

🗣️ 口语

我们与他人交流以表达我们的想法并了解他人的想法。游戏和配对活动中的语言表现是学习和说语言的鼓励来源。

👂 听力

通过专注于让学生听该特定语言的声音，可以增强听力技能。这将帮助他们正确发音。聆听不同背景和地区的人的讲话将帮助他们理解不同的口音和单词的准确发音。

Our Teaching Approach

📖 Reading

Reading helps improve all parts of the English language – vocabulary, spelling, grammar, and writing. It develops the learner's intuition for the correct forms of the language. Reading skills help students grasp content and draw conclusions.

✍️ Writing

Writing provides a learner with physical evidence of achievement. It helps to consolidate a grasp of vocabulary and structure, how to understand text and write compositions. It fosters the learner's ability to summarize and use the language freely.

🗣️ Speaking

We communicate with others to express our ideas and know others' ideas as well. Language activities in games and pair work are an encouraging source to learn and speak the language.

👂 Listening

Listening skills can be enhanced by focusing on making students listen to the sounds of the particular language. This helps them with the right pronunciation of words. Listening to speech from different backgrounds and regions helps them understand different accents.
